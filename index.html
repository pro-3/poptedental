Our Services

Complete dental care, under one roof

Affordable treatment for every stage of oral health — from prevention to restoration.

General Consultation

A thorough dental check-up to catch problems early, with honest, easy-to-understand advice on the right treatment.

Root Canal Treatment (RCT)

Pain-free root canal treatment that saves your natural tooth and stops infection from spreading further.

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-looking replacement for missing teeth, restoring both your bite and your confidence.

Tooth Extraction

Safe, gentle removal of damaged or wisdom teeth, done carefully to keep discomfort to a minimum.

Teeth Cleaning (Ultrasonic Scaling)

Deep cleaning that removes plaque and tartar buildup, leaving your teeth fresh, smooth and healthier.

Cosmetic Tooth Filling

Tooth-coloured fillings that repair cavities invisibly, blending naturally with the rest of your smile.

Teeth Whitening

A safe, professional whitening treatment that brightens your smile in a single visit to the clinic.

Dentures & Fixed Teeth

Comfortable removable and fixed denture options for patients who have lost one or more teeth.

Preventive Dental Care

Guidance on brushing, diet and habits that protect your teeth long-term, for both children and adults.
